### **Figure 1: 六大维度投资分布饼图**

#### 📍 论文位置
**Section 4.2.1 维度投资战略布局**

#### 🎯 图表作用
1. **展示整体战略**: 直观呈现1万亿投资在6个维度的分配比例
2. **突出重点领域**: 通过扇形大小对比，强调基础设施优先策略
3. **量化投资决策**: 精确标注每个维度的投资额和占比

#### 📖 图注 (Caption)
```
Figure 1. Strategic Investment Distribution Across Six AI Dimensions

The pie chart illustrates the allocation of 10,000 billion RMB across six 
strategic dimensions. Infrastructure dominates with 32.33% (3,233 billion RMB), 
reflecting a hardware-first strategy. Policy and Talent receive equal emphasis 
(17.39% each), demonstrating long-term commitment. The exploded slice highlights 
the infrastructure investment as the cornerstone of China's AI development.

饼图展示了1万亿元人民币在六大战略维度的分配情况。基础设施以32.33%
（3233亿元）占主导地位，体现了硬件优先战略。政策和人才获得同等重视
（各占17.39%），体现长期承诺。突出的扇形强调基础设施投资是中国AI
发展的基石。
```

#### 🔍 图表解读

**核心发现**:
- **基础设施主导** (32.33%): 算力建设是短期突破的关键
- **政策与人才并重** (各17.39%): 制度保障与人力资源的双驱动
- **应用与研发平衡** (15.12% + 12.65%): 市场驱动与创新驱动结合
- **产出适度投入** (5.12%): 开源生态的培育性投资

**战略意义**:
```
基础设施 (I) → 快速见效，3-5年内提升算力
政策 (P)     → 中期见效，5-10年内完善法规
人才 (T)     → 长期见效，10-20年内培育梯队
```

#### 📖 图注 (Caption)
```
Figure 3. Growth Rate Analysis for 24 AI Indicators Post-Investment

by magnitude: extreme growth (>1000%, green), high growth (500-1000%, light
```

#### 📖 图注 (Caption)
```
Figure 4. Top Indicators Investment Comparison Across Six Dimensions

The grouped bar chart compares the top 4 indicators within each of the six 
dimensions on a unified Y-axis. Each dimension uses a distinct color scheme 
with gradient intensity for ranking. Infrastructure dominates with GPU Clusters 
(1,500B RMB) and TOP500 Systems (732B RMB). Vertical dashed lines separate

This reveals intra-dimensional investment structure and cross-dimensional 
coordination.

分组条形图在统一Y轴上比较六个维度内的前4项指标。每个维度使用独特的
```

#### 🔍 图表解读

**维度内部结构**:

| 维度 | Top1指标 | 投资额 | 占该维度比例 |
|------|---------|--------|-------------|
| **基础设施** | GPU集群 | 1500B¥ | 46.4% |
| **人才** | 顶尖学者 | 821B¥ | 47.2% |
| **政策** | AI政策数量 | 1474B¥ | 84.8% |
| **研发** | 企业研发 | 928B¥ | 73.4% |
| **应用** | AI市场规模 | 1121B¥ | 74.1% |
| **产出** | GitHub项目 | 309B¥ | 60.4% |

**发现**:
1. **投资集中度差异**:
   - 政策维度: 高度集中（Top1占84.8%）→ 政策制定是核心
   - 基础设施: 相对分散（Top1占46.4%）→ 多元化布局

2. **跨维度对比**:
   - 基础设施的Top1 (1500B¥) > 人才维度总投资 (1739B¥)
   - 证明算力优先战略的力度

3. **协同效应**:
   ```
   基础设施 → 支撑 → 研发 → 驱动 → 应用 → 产生 → 产出
   ```

#### 📖 图注 (Caption)
```
Figure 5. Investment Efficiency Bubble Chart with Four-Dimensional Analysis

The bubble chart visualizes investment efficiency across four dimensions: 
X-axis (investment amount), Y-axis (growth rate, logarithmic scale), bubble 
size (absolute growth), and color (dimension category). Dashed red lines mark 
median values, creating four quadrants. The top-right quadrant represents 
"High Investment, High Return" zone, containing GPU Clusters, AI Policies, 
and AI Market Size. GitHub Projects appears in the top-left as a high-leverage 
outlier (low investment, extreme growth). This chart identifies optimal
```
